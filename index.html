Fully Automatic Telescopic Seating

The RCS system represents the pinnacle of automated venue seating. Our fully automatic telescopic seating opens and closes via remote switch—delivering uncompromising functionality and safety for theaters, arenas, schools, and convention halls.

The advanced Drum Unit (DU) non-friction drive system eliminates direct load on the floor, while sensor-equipped front panels and interlock mechanisms ensure superior safety in every venue configuration.

The Original Sleep Capsule

In 1979, Kotobuki invented the capsule bed, revolutionizing hospitality and corporate rest spaces. Inspired by architect Kisho Kurokawa's visionary "Capsule House" at the 1970 Osaka Expo, our capsule beds have become a cultural icon of modern Japan.

From the sleek DREAM-C series to the gold-standard SPACE D with 36 customization combinations, each capsule is engineered with fire-resistant materials and available in multiple configurations. Our VISIT-E stand-up capsule rooms and B-CUBE budget line serve diverse market needs globally.
