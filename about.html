K+ Barcelona Innovation Lab

[K+ Barcelona Facility]

Where the Future is Designed

Our innovation lab in Barcelona represents the cutting edge of seating innovation and sustainable manufacturing. Here, scientists, designers, and engineers collaborate to develop the next generation of products that define our industry.

At K+, we pioneer research into sustainable materials, including recycled PET upholstery and environmentally-conscious manufacturing processes. We work with leading academic institutions and sustainability experts to ensure every innovation meets the highest environmental standards.

The lab serves as our global center for product development, materials research, design testing, and operational optimization. It embodies our commitment to continuous improvement and our vision of a more sustainable future for public spaces.

Our Manufacturing Philosophy

Three Core Principles Guide All We Create

Universal Design

Every product is engineered to be accessible and comfortable for people of all ages, abilities, and body types. We believe great design serves everyone.

Ecology

Environmental responsibility is integrated into every decision. From material sourcing to end-of-life recyclability, we minimize our impact on the planet.

Long Life

We build for decades, not years. Our products are engineered for durability and designed for maintenance, providing exceptional value over extended lifecycles.
