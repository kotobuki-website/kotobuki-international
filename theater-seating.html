Japanese Craftsmanship Since 1914

Precision Engineering Meets Artistic Vision

Collaboration with the World's Top Architects

For over a century, Kotobuki has partnered with the world's leading architects and designers to create theater seating that transcends function and becomes art. Every seat is engineered to perfection with meticulous attention to comfort, durability, and aesthetic harmony.

Our commitment to Japanese precision manufacturing ensures that every detail—from frame construction to upholstery finishing—meets the highest international standards. We don't just build seats; we craft experiences.

Each installation reflects our philosophy: creating spaces where audiences can fully immerse themselves in the performance, undistracted by discomfort or inferior design.

Sustainability & Innovation

Leading the Industry in Eco-Conscious Manufacturing

K+ Barcelona

Our innovation lab in Barcelona drives cutting-edge research into sustainable materials, manufacturing processes, and circular design principles. Here we collaborate with scientists and designers to invent the future of seating.

Recycled PET Upholstery

We pioneered the use of recycled PET materials in theatrical upholstery, transforming plastic waste into luxurious, durable fabrics. This reduces our environmental impact without compromising on quality or comfort.

Eco-Design Methodology

Every product is designed with the environment in mind. From material selection to end-of-life recyclability, we consider the complete lifecycle impact of our seating systems.

Component-Level Recyclability

Our modular design approach means individual components can be easily replaced, repaired, or recycled separately, extending product life and minimizing waste.

Renewal Services

Give Your Venue a New Lease on Life

[Renewal Service Process]

Next-Generation Renewal System

Rather than replacing entire seating systems, our innovative renewal program allows venues to refresh their spaces by updating specific components while reusing and repurposing materials.

Our sustainability-focused approach means existing frames can be refurbished with new upholstery, mechanisms, and accessories. This reduces waste, saves costs, and allows venues to extend the life of their Kotobuki installations for decades.

We handle the entire process—from assessment to installation—ensuring your renewed venue looks and performs like new while maintaining its commitment to environmental responsibility.
