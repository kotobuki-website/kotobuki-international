The Challenge

Project Overview

Arts United Center serves as the cultural heart of Fort Wayne, hosting a diverse program of ballet, opera, Broadway touring productions, and community events. The venue required seating that could match the prestige of the programming while meeting the specific acoustic and sightline requirements of a multi-discipline performing arts hall.

Color consistency across multiple seating types — from orchestra stalls to balcony tiers — was a critical requirement, as was durability suited to the high usage demands of a community arts center.

Our Solution

What We Delivered

Kotobuki delivered a bespoke seating installation across all seating areas of Arts United Center, with upholstery and finish selections developed in close collaboration with the venue's design team. Each seat was engineered to contribute positively to the acoustic character of the hall while providing exceptional comfort for long-form performances.

Our ability to maintain color consistency and material matching across different seating tiers and types ensured a unified visual experience throughout the auditorium.

The Result

Outcome

The Arts United Center seating installation has been praised by patrons and artists alike for its comfort, elegance, and the way it complements the architectural spirit of the building. The venue continues to be a flagship example of how thoughtfully designed seating elevates the performing arts experience.
