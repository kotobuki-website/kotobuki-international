The Only Fully Automatic System

Revolutionary Control System (RCS) Technology

Seamless Automation

The Kotobuki RCS (Revolutionary Control System) represents the pinnacle of seating automation. With a single remote switch, entire stadium systems deploy automatically, providing unprecedented convenience and reliability.

Engineered for venues requiring maximum capacity flexibility, the RCS system combines advanced hydraulic technology with precision engineering to deliver smooth, reliable operation every single time.

Experience the future of crowd management with technology that thinks ahead of your needs.

[RCS Technology Diagram]

How It Works

A 4-Step Process to Complete Deployment

1

Remote Switch

Operator activates system with single remote control

2

Drum Unit Drive

Motor engages hydraulic drum units beneath stage

3

Platforms Extend

Seating platforms smoothly extend into venue space

4

Seats Deploy

Individual seats lock into position automatically
